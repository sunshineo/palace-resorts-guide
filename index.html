Preparation

Before You Go

A little prep goes a long way. Do these things before you leave home.

📱

Download the App

Get the Palace Resorts App on your phone. It's essential — restaurants, room service, concierge, activities, and hailing golf carts are all in there.

💵

Bring Tip Money

~$200 for a week: $100 in singles, a $20, and the rest in $5 bills. Tipping isn't required, but it makes a noticeable difference in service — especially with concierge and pool staff.

💳

No Foreign Transaction Fees

Bring a credit card with no foreign transaction fees. You'll use it for the 16% service charge on credits and any extras.

👶

Traveling with Kids?

No need to bring a crib or stroller — the resort provides them free. Heads up: the strollers are pretty bare-minimum.

Arrival

Getting There & Checking In

🛬 At the Airport

After customs, you'll enter a "waiting" area where people try to lure you with "free" offers. Ignore everyone. Go directly outside to find your resort transportation — look for staff in khaki, sometimes holding signs with your name or "Palace Resorts." It's a shared shuttle, 15–20 min ride.

💎 Diamond Elite Check-In

You have Diamond Elite status — the highest level. Don't wait in normal check-in lines! Look for the dedicated Diamond area. Bellmen will direct you.

📍 Nizuc Check-In

Walk right through the main doors. The Diamond check-in is through a set of glass doors.

📍 Sunrise Check-In

Walk past the escalator on the left. The check-in area is to the left of the escalators, down a hallway. You'll see a photo area on your right.

💡 Pro Tip

If you get a VIP band instead of a Diamond Elite band (they have a limit), you'll check in at the normal front area instead. Either way, you get the same perks.

Understanding Your Credits

The Credit System

A week stay gets you 1,500 credits. Here's how they break down.

🧖 Spa Credits 750
🏄 Activity Credits 750
Important: You still pay 16% service charge on everything you use credits for. For example, a couples massage costing 600 credits = $96 at checkout + tip. Once credits are used up, you pay full price.

Relaxation

Spa Guide

Three spas across the property, but one stands above the rest.

⭐

The Grand Spa

The best and biggest spa on the property. This is the only one worth visiting. Book your spa services here so you can enjoy the Hydrotherapy area before or after.

🏌️

Golf Course Spa

Medium-sized. Fine, but skip it — The Grand is better in every way.

🌊

Sunrise Spa

The smallest of the three. Same recommendation: head to The Grand instead.

💆 Spa Tips

Free mani/pedi: Diamond Elite members get one complimentary set — book it on day 1 or 2 through your concierge.

Hydrotherapy: Walk in anytime at The Grand, no reservation needed.

Remember: Spa services use your 750 spa credits + the 16% service charge. See the Credit System section for details.

On the Green

Golf

⛳

Complimentary Golf Access

With Diamond Elite, you get unlimited golf at the resort. Three 9-hole courses, about a 7-minute cart ride from your room.

  • Bring your own clubs or rent for ~$60/day
  • Play as much or as little as you want
  • Must book using golf weeks — let the host know before you arrive if interested, since these are booked differently than regular stays
Things to Do

Activities & Excursions

🎢

On & Off-Resort

There's plenty to do — the aqua park in Cancún proper (jet skis, shark ride) is a blast. Tulum and Aztec ruins are amazing but expect 1+ hour drive each way.

  • Always ask concierge about drive time before booking
  • Expect park entrance fees for off-resort activities
  • Some excursions have transportation fees on top
  • Activity credits cover resort-based activities
Day 1 & 2

Settling In

No need to rush. Get oriented, then plan your week.

👋 Meet Your Concierge

You'll be assigned one at check-in. Use them for everything — cabana reservations, restaurant bookings, spa appointments, activities. Call or use the in-app concierge.

🍽️ Book All Dinners

Once you've picked your restaurants (check the app), have your concierge book all dinner reservations in one go. Aim for 6–8pm. Then you're set for the week.

😌 Take It Easy

If you're starving during check-in, ask to do a quick check-in, go eat, and come back for activity/spa bookings later. There's no rush.

Departure

Check-Out

🚐

Transportation

Confirm your return shuttle date and time with your concierge. Plan to leave the resort ~3 hours before your flight.

💳

Settle Up

At checkout, they'll settle your spa and activity charges (the 16% service fees). They'll cut off your wristbands and give you a checkout pass — you can still eat at restaurants if you have time.

Questions

FAQ

Does the resort do their own excursions?
Ask your concierge — they can book resort-organized excursions as well as third-party ones. Resort excursions often include transportation.
How much is parking if I rent a car?
Check with the front desk on arrival. Rates can vary, but many guests use resort transportation and shuttles instead.
What are all the excursion options and where can I find details?
The Palace Resorts app has a full list of available excursions. Your concierge can also walk you through options, prices, and drive times.
